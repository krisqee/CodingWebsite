You're Dicking With Me

October 21st, 2014 by k

Since the Critic released his editorial, nearly all of his fans have been discussing what in the hell could be so good that he teased it in such a way. And it looked like it was gonna be cool, with the set-up and everything. But...well. It didn't.

So. Um. Yeah. Monster Squad. I mean, I had an idea that it might've been it, especially with the Critic's huge tie with the 80's and 80's nostalgia (yeah, no shit) but...I gotta say, it definitely wasn't one of my favorite reviews. For several reasons.

So we get a pretty cool beginning, with the 'Token Team' giving us the basic formula of the main 80's protagonists. But, well. It kinda turned into the Disney Afternoon of NC Reboot episodes. Except they wanted to try and give it a plot. A really half-assed plot. I would understand it if they wanted to go with the movie, but...still. It was really just kinda dull. Even when they were reviewing the movie it wasn't that exciting. The 'villain' was stupid. Everythng was just stupid.

Reality Sucks

If you're gonna introduce a villain, don't just...do that. It doesn't matter that it's a one-off. It's still a character, nevertheless. Everyone expected the audience to hate Tamara, and if you pulled the same shit on her introduction as you did with Reality's, then people would've hated the shit out of her. Seriously, even the fucking seahorses from the Room review had a better character than this chickenshit.
